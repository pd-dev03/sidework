The Origin
Story.

by Priyanshu Dave

At first it was just for me. I needed a portfolio — fast. Every drag-and-drop builder felt like assembling IKEA furniture with missing instructions. So I built my own. Clean. Simple. Done in minutes. Then I realized — I'm not alone. There are thousands of busy, perfection-phobic folks who need a quick, elegant resume site without selling their soul or their wallet. And that's how BuildMyFolio became the answer to "Ugh, not another PDF!"
