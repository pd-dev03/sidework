01 / Origin Story

Built from
Real Frustration.

— Priyanshu Dave, Creator

I needed a portfolio. Fast. Every builder I touched felt like assembling IKEA furniture with pages missing from the manual — drag, drop, break, cry, repeat. So I built my own system. Clean. Fast. Live in five minutes. Then it hit me — I wasn't alone. Thousands of busy, talented people need a sharp professional portfolio without burning their evenings or emptying their wallets. That's how BuildMyFolio became the definitive answer to "Ugh, not another PDF."
