Who I am
I'm Priyanshu,
the person behind
BuildMyFolio.

I'm a developer, designer, and builder from India. I built BuildMyFolio because I was tired of watching talented people struggle to present themselves online. A great portfolio should take minutes — not a weekend of tutorials and broken drag-and-drop builders.

My mission is simple: make it effortless for anyone — engineers, designers, managers, freelancers — to have a portfolio they're proud to share. Zero code. Personal URL. Live in minutes.
