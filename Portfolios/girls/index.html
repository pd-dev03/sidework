My Story

I'm Sophia, a passionate UI/UX designer and frontend developer with over 5 years of experience in creating digital products that users love. My journey began with a degree in Graphic Design, which evolved into a deep interest in human-computer interaction and user experience.

I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics—it's about solving real problems and creating meaningful experiences. My approach combines user research, data analysis, and creative design thinking to deliver solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with startups, agencies, and large corporations, helping them create digital experiences that drive engagement and business growth.
