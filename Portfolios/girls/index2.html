About Me

Get to know me better - my journey, passions, and the values that drive my work

Hello! I'm Isabella, a passionate creative designer and frontend developer with over 5 years of experience crafting beautiful digital experiences. I believe in the power of design to solve problems and create meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.

My journey started with a degree in Visual Arts, which gave me a strong foundation in aesthetics and design principles. I then expanded my skills into web development, learning to bring my designs to life with code. This unique combination allows me to bridge the gap between design and development seamlessly.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me exploring art galleries, practicing calligraphy, or enjoying a good cup of tea while reading design blogs. I'm constantly learning and experimenting with new technologies to stay at the forefront of digital design.
