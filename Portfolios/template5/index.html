About

A creative mind
driven by purpose.

I am a creative director with twelve years of experience turning ambiguous briefs into razor-sharp brand stories. My work lives at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics — I believe a great brand is one you feel before you understand. From global corporations to beloved independents, my clients share one thing: they never look like anyone else. I am Paris-based and obsessed with craft, detail, and the invisible architecture that makes design work.
